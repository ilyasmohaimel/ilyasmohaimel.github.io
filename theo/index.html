§ 01 / About

More than a YouTuber.

He turns opinions about software into actual products, open-source tools, and companies.

Theo previously worked as a software engineer at Twitch. He later founded Ping Labs, part of Y Combinator's Winter 2022 batch.

Today, his work spans AI chat, developer infrastructure, open-source starters, and tools for coding agents. He is also strongly associated with the T3 Stack, an opinionated approach to building typesafe full-stack TypeScript applications.
